Year Up

Closing the opportunity divide

Year Up is a one-year, intensive training program that provides urban young adults 18-24, with a unique combination of technical and professional skills, college credits, an educational stipend, and a corporate internship. We prepare our students in the Information Technology (IT), Project Management, Investment Operations, and Client Services fields.
